What is Navagraha Shanti Pooja?

The Navagraha Shanti Pooja is a powerful Vedic ritual dedicated to pacifying all nine celestial planets — Surya, Chandra, Mangal, Budh, Brihaspati, Shukra, Shani, Rahu, and Ketu.

When one or more planets are malefic or weak in your birth chart, they can create obstacles in career, relationships, health, and finances. This pooja neutralizes their negative effects and strengthens their positive influence.
