Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our services, astrology, and spiritual guidance.

What is Vedic Astrology (Jyotish Shastra)?

Vedic Astrology, also known as Jyotish Shastra, is an ancient Indian science of light that uses the positions of planets and stars at the time of your birth to provide insights into your life, personality, career, health, relationships, and spiritual growth. It is rooted in the Vedas and has been practiced for thousands of years.

How accurate are your astrology reports?

Our reports are prepared by experienced astrologers using classical Vedic methods from authentic texts like Brihat Parashara Hora Shastra. With accurate birth details (date, time, and place), our predictions are highly precise. We have a 4.9★ rating from over 50,000+ satisfied clients.

How can I book a consultation with an astrologer?

You can book a consultation directly through our website by visiting the Consultation page, or you can message us on WhatsApp. We offer sessions with Team Astrologers, Senior Astrologers, and direct consultations with Acharya Ji. All sessions are available via audio/video call.

Are your gemstones and Rudrakshas certified?

Yes, all our gemstones are 100% natural, lab-certified, and come with a certificate of authenticity. Our Rudrakshas are sourced from authentic Nepali and Indonesian origins. Every product is energized through Vedic mantras before delivery and comes with wearing/usage instructions.

Can I watch the Pooja being performed online?

Absolutely! We provide live streaming for all major Poojas. You can participate from anywhere in the world and watch the entire ritual being performed by experienced Brahmins at sacred temples. A video recording is also shared after the Pooja is completed.

What information do I need to provide for a Kundli report?

To prepare an accurate Kundli report, we need your exact date of birth, time of birth, and place of birth. The more precise the birth time, the more accurate your chart analysis will be. If you don't know your exact birth time, our astrologers can use Prashna Kundli methods.
